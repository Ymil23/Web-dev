Secret "Liquid Gold" Mac

This recipe bypasses the traditional roux to prevent graininess, using a chemistry trick for a perfectly emulsified sauce.

The Classified Ingredients

  • 1 lb Cavatappi pasta (the ridges hold sauce better)
  • 12 oz Evaporated milk Secret #1
  • 1/2 tsp Sodium Citrate Secret #2: The Emulsifier
  • 1 lb Sharp White Cheddar (hand-grated only)
  • 1 tsp Smoked Paprika
  • 1 tbsp Cold Butter

Standard Operating Procedure

  1. Cook pasta in highly salted water until just shy of al dente.
  2. In a medium saucepan, whisk the sodium citrate into the evaporated milk and bring to a simmer.
  3. Slowly whisk in the cheese one handful at a time until the sauce is impossibly smooth.
  4. Stir in the smoked paprika and cold butter for a glossy finish.
  5. Combine pasta and sauce immediately.
